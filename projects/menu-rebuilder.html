The Old Way: Paper & PDFs

Most restaurants (especially in India) use paper menus or poorly formatted PDFs. They are hard to update, unreadable on mobile, lack appetizing descriptions, and look unprofessional to modern diners.

The New Way: AI Digital Menus

Our AI pipeline reads a photo of any menu, extracts the items, uses LLMs to write mouth-watering descriptions, and outputs a premium, branded, mobile-first HTML menu complete with a printable QR code.
