The Challenge

Why Businesses Need an AI Review Agent

The Problem

  • 88% of customers check reviews before buying — unanswered = lost trust
  • Negative reviews sit for days destroying reputation silently
  • Manual replies cost $400–900/month in staff time
  • Generic "Thanks for your review" replies feel robotic and hurt more than help

Our Solution

  • Monitors Google Business Profile 24/7 for new reviews
  • GPT-4o writes genuinely personalized replies — never sounds like a bot
  • Positive reviews → auto-published instantly (28s avg)
  • Negative reviews → drafted, escalated to owner for approval

System Design

Review Pipeline Architecture

New Review Google Business
n8n Trigger Webhook / Poll
GPT-4o Drafts Personalized reply
Sentiment Check Positive / Negative
Publish / Escalate Auto or approval

Under the Hood

The 4 n8n Workflows Powering the Agent

1

Review Monitor

Polls Google Business Profile API every 5 minutes for new reviews. Detects new entries by comparing against the last-seen review ID stored in n8n's static data. Triggers the rest of the pipeline only when a genuinely new review arrives.

CRON → /review-monitor
2

Sentiment Analysis + Reply Generation

Sends the review text to GPT-4o with a system prompt that includes the business name, owner name, and tone guidelines. The model classifies sentiment (positive/neutral/negative) and generates a reply that references specific details from the review — never generic.

POST /generate-reply
3

Smart Router

Routes based on sentiment: ★★★★-★★★★★ reviews are auto-published via Google Business Profile API. ★-★★★ reviews are held as drafts and sent to the owner via Slack or WhatsApp for approval before publishing.

IF → /auto-publish OR /escalate
4

Weekly Digest

Every Monday at 9am, compiles a report of all reviews received, replies sent, sentiment distribution, and rating trend. Sends a formatted summary to the owner's email with a Google Sheets link for the full log.

CRON → /weekly-digest
