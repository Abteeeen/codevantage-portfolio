Challenge & Approach

Manual Prospecting Is Broken

The Problem

Manual lead hunting consumes 3–4 hours every day. BDE reps scroll through hundreds of hiring posts, guess email addresses with no verification, and send generic outreach that gets ignored.

  • Hundreds of posts scanned manually each morning
  • Email addresses guessed — no SMTP verification
  • Generic copy-paste outreach with low response rates
  • High-value targets buried in noise

The Solution

An AI agent scans 378+ hiring posts per run, extracts company details, verifies emails via SMTP code 250, and drafts personalized 4-sentence outreach — all autonomously.

  • Automated scanning of HN "Who is Hiring" threads & job boards
  • AI/ML keyword filtering and company size analysis
  • SMTP handshake verification (code 250 = confirmed valid)
  • GPT-generated personalized emails referencing specific hiring needs

Pipeline Breakdown

Four Steps to Qualified Outreach

1

Source Scanning

The agent crawls Hacker News "Who is Hiring" monthly threads, job board aggregators, and LinkedIn postings. Each run processes 378+ individual hiring posts, extracting raw company names, roles, and technology mentions.

2

AI/ML Company Filtering

Posts are scored against AI/ML keyword dictionaries — matching terms like "LLM," "computer vision," "MLOps," and "fine-tuning." Additional filters evaluate company size signals and technology stack mentions to surface only relevant targets.

3

Email Discovery & SMTP Verification

For each qualified company, the agent generates candidate email addresses using common patterns (first@company.com, f.last@company.com, first.last@company.com). Each address is verified via SMTP handshake — only addresses returning code 250 are marked as confirmed valid.

4

Personalized Outreach Drafting

GPT-4 generates concise 4-sentence emails that reference the company's specific hiring needs, tech stack, and growth signals. Each draft is unique — no templates, no generic pitches. A human reviews and approves before sending.

Email Verification

SMTP Handshake Protocol

SMTP Verification Flow
SEND HELO verify.codevantage.ai Handshake
RECV 220 mx.company.com ESMTP ready Connected
SEND MAIL FROM:<verify@codevantage.ai> Sender
RECV 250 OK Accepted
SEND RCPT TO:<cto@targetcompany.com> Verify
RECV 250 Recipient OK Verified
Why SMTP verification matters: Pattern-based email guessing alone has a 40–60% bounce rate. By performing a live SMTP handshake up to the RCPT TO stage (without sending any email), we confirm the mailbox exists — bringing bounce rates near zero.
Whale Hunter Report: The agent also produces a separate "Whale Hunter" report that identifies high-value enterprise targets — companies with 50+ employees, multiple AI roles open, and Series B+ funding — flagged for premium, high-touch outreach by senior BDE reps.
