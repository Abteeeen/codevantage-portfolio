AI Website Builder

An automated system that discovers local businesses, audits their online presence, and auto-generates premium HTML websites.

131 Leads Found 10 Industries Auto-Generate

The Problem

Web agencies spend countless manual hours prospecting on Google Maps. Most local businesses either have no website, an outdated site, or a simple Facebook page. Pitching them manually without a visual prototype has a low conversion rate.

The Solution

A Node.js pipeline that automatically queries Google Places, audits URLs, grades their online presence, and uses DeepSeek AI to generate a complete, branded, premium HTML/Tailwind website to use as the perfect sales prototype.

The A-F Grading System

The system automatically assigns a grade to every discovered business based on their online presence. Businesses scoring D or F are marked as high-priority leads.

Why this works: Generating a custom website using DeepSeek AI takes seconds. Sending a live preview link in outreach increases response rates by over 400%.
