The Problem

Generic outbound outreach has less than a 2% response rate. Finding people who actually need your product right now requires manually reading thousands of posts in Facebook groups and subreddits to find specific complaints and buying signals.

The Solution

A multi-agent crew built with CrewAI that monitors communities 24/7. It uses natural language processing to extract the exact pain points mentioned, scores leads from 1-10 based on urgency, and prepares a personalized message referencing their exact problem.

AI Agent Roles

Role: Analyzer

Role: Lead Scorer

Intent Scorer

Analyzes linguistic urgency. "I hate X" = Score 4. "Need alternative to X immediately, budget approved" = Score 9.
