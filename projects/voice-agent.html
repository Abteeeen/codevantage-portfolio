Under the Hood

The 4 n8n Workflows Powering Maya

1

Check Availability

When a patient asks for an appointment, Maya triggers this workflow to query the doctor's real-time schedule. Returns available time slots filtered by doctor speciality and preferred date.

POST /check-availability
2

Book Appointment

Once the patient confirms a slot, this workflow creates the appointment record, generates a unique appointment ID (e.g., APT-MQ9684CE), and returns a confirmation message with all details.

POST /book-appointment
3

Clinic FAQ

Handles common questions like clinic hours, location, accepted insurance, parking info, and more. Pulls answers from a curated knowledge base of 12 FAQ entries for instant, accurate responses.

POST /clinic-faq
4

Call Summary

Fires automatically when a call ends. Generates a structured summary of the conversation — patient intent, actions taken, follow-ups needed — and sends it via webhook to the clinic's dashboard.

POST /call-summary
